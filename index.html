About

With a passion to combine whole body mental health & the arts, Violetta did her 200 hours Alliance Teacher Training at the loveliest of studios at Yoga On The Lane, learning from Naomi Annand & Adam Hocke. With a degree in Psychology, she is currently in training to become a drama & movement therapist.

About

Having experienced complicated grief and trauma herself, she is passionate about holding space for others to help them work through difficult life experiences. As the physical practice of yoga asanas helped her soothe her nervous system and reconnect back into her body, so did the teachings of yoga aid her mental wellbeing. She is therefore keen to support each and every individual on their journey to finding home in their own skin with the yogic tools of breath, movement and meditation.
